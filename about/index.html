About SolarPower101

Independent solar guidance, built for homeowners

SolarPower101 helps homeowners learn the essentials, check their own numbers, compare options, and request a private project review — without being sold to.

Why this exists

The residential solar industry runs on lead generation. Homeowners who fill out a "get quotes" form often find their contact details sold to multiple installers before they've had a chance to learn what solar actually involves.

SolarPower101 was built to flip that model. We start with education, not sales. Every tool and lesson is free, requires no account, and stores nothing about you unless you choose to create an account later. When you're ready, your project review request goes to a review queue — not a call list.

How we work

Education first

Every lesson and tool is designed to help you understand solar on your own terms before anyone tries to sell you anything. No jargon, no pressure, no account required.

Private by design

Your contact details stay private. We never sell leads, share your information with installers, or display your data back to you after submission. An account is always optional.

Independent guidance

We don't take commissions from installers, rank partners, or steer you toward preferred companies. Every tool and lesson exists to help you make your own informed decision.

Free, always

No paywalls, no subscriptions, no lead selling. The lessons, planning tools, and project review are free for every homeowner.

How it started

SolarPower101 was created because the residential solar industry makes it hard for homeowners to get straightforward answers. Most "get quotes" sites are lead-generation engines that sell your contact details to multiple installers before you've had a chance to understand what you need.

We wanted something different: a place where homeowners can learn at their own pace, check real sun data, compare options privately, and only share their details when they're ready. No lead selling. No pressure. No account required.

Built by a small team

SolarPower101 is a small independent project. We're not backed by an installer network, a lead-generation platform, or a utility company. That independence is the whole point: it lets us put homeowners first.
