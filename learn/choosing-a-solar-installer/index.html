Choosing a Solar Installer

Compare installers with practical questions about licensing, design assumptions, equipment, warranties, and communication.

8 minBook chapter

Practical steps

  1. Ask each installer to explain system size, estimated production, and assumptions in plain language.
  2. Compare warranties, payment terms, cancellation terms, and who performs the installation.
  3. Avoid treating estimated savings as a guarantee.
