Check roof and property readiness

Review roof age, shade, available space, and ownership details so a quote conversation starts with the right context.

Practical steps

  1. Estimate your roof age and note any repairs planned in the next few years.
  2. Look for shade from trees, chimneys, neighboring buildings, or vents during the day.
  3. Confirm who can approve changes to the property before scheduling a site review.
