Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

What we collect

You can use most of SolarPower101 without providing any personal information. Lessons, solar tools, irradiance lookup, roof pre-check, and the assistant are all free and anonymous.

We collect personal information only when you choose to provide it:

  • Project inquiry form: name, email address, phone number, property address, and project details you submit when requesting a project review.
  • Account registration: name and email address when you create an optional account.
  • Onboarding forms: utility usage, solar estimates, readiness assessments, financing details, quotes, project tracking, warranty records, production data, maintenance reminders, battery plans, EV charger plans, carbon footprint data, and contract review details you save to your account.
  • Installer onboarding: company details, contact information, service areas, and credentials submitted by installers seeking directory listing.

How we use it

  • Process and respond to your project review request.
  • Provide the account features and tools you choose to use.
  • Review and approve installer directory listings.
  • Improve the site and fix issues.

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We do not share your contact details with installers unless you explicitly submit a lead request through the platform. We do not use your data for advertising.

How we store it

Data is stored in Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database, and may be cached in our content delivery network. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication when you are logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. No cookies are set for anonymous visitors.

Third-party services

  • Supabase: database, authentication, and storage. See Supabase Privacy.
  • GitHub Pages: static site hosting. See GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • NASA POWER: public solar irradiance data used for non-persistent lookup tools. No personal data is sent to NASA.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hellosolarpower101@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hellosolarpower101@gmail.com.
